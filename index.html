About Me

Hi, it's Rahatara here — I hold a doctorate in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Ottawa, where I focused on building AI-powered Digital Twins for real-world challenges in railway safety and healthcare. I’ve collaborated with the National Research Council Canada and presented my research at top international conferences. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and edge deployment — and I'm passionate about building technology that’s both cutting-edge and impactful.

I’ve published over 20 peer-reviewed papers with 500+ citations, taught AI and Python programming, and regularly speak at DevFest, Women in Tech, and other community conferences. I'm currently looking for opportunities where I can bring AI research into production and scale real-world solutions.
